Who I Am

Engineer.
Innovator. Builder.

I'm Lwanga Stefan Gobero, a first-year BSc Mechatronics and Robotics Engineering student at Uganda Christian University (UCU), Mukono. I'm currently completing my Workshop Practice programme at the Uganda Industrial Research Institute (UIRI).

My engineering passion lives at the intersection of automation, embedded systems, and human-centred design. My flagship venture is Mama Uhai — a maternal healthcare mobile application I've pitched at the UICT Innovation Hub in Nakawa, designed to address Uganda's maternal mortality crisis through technology.

I'm an active voice in Uganda's growing tech and innovation ecosystem — attending events like AI Agent Con Mukono and documenting my journey through Medium and LinkedIn. I believe African engineers must solve African problems.
